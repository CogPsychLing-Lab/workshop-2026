Hands-on Workshop

Introduction to PsychoPy & EEG

for Cognitive Science & Psycholinguistics

This 3-day workshop introduces key methods in psycholinguistics with an emphasis on practical skills required to conduct experimental research. Participants will gain hands-on experience in designing experiments, collecting EEG data and analyzing results.
